Montral Space Symposium 2017

The first Montreal Space Symposium (MSS) took place October 5th and 6th 2017, and served as a public conference to discuss, address, and plan for the future of space in Montreal and Canada. MSS showcased a plethora of recent contributions and advancements in the Canadian space industry and Canadian space research.

Fact Sheet

  • Who - 350 Industry professionals, reaearchers, students and enthusiasts attended the event
  • What - 52 talks organized around 4 subjects, from science and engineering to law.
  • Where - Palais des Congrès
  • When - October 5th & 6th, 2017

FAQ

Generic Questions About This Event

1 Who can attend?

Anybody! This event is targetting students, professionals as well as the general public.

2 How much is the event?

The day part of the event is completely free. The evening events, (the banquet and the Wine and Cheese) will be extra (cost TBD).

3 Is there food available?

Palais des Congres has several restaurants on the ground floor, you'll find something to satisfy your taste buds.

4 Is there transportation and/or parking?

Please use public transit to make it to the venue. Bixi racks are located all around the building. You can access the Palais des Congres directly from Place d'Armes metro station, or walk 10 minutes from the Gare Centrale

Paid parking is also available:

  • Indoor parking lot (400 spots) on Chenneville Street, via Viger Ave
  • Indoor parking lot (1 200 spots) accessible via St. Antoine Street.

5 How do I get involved in space development?

You will have to attend the MSS to get an answer to that one. Come get insight from industry leader on how to get involved and make an impact in the space industry.

6 What types of information is covered?

A various spectrum of subjects will be covered, from "what is space" to the newest groundbreaking technologies being developped. More details to come soon!

7 Who is the Montreal Students Space Association?

The MSSA is a regroupment of student space associations across Montreal universities. We united under the common goal of promoting space related discussions and awareness to the public.

8 How can we help or sponsor the event?

Shoot an email to montrealspacesymposium@gmail.com to take part in this unique adventure.
