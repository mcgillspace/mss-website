Event Overview

The Montreal Space Symposium (MSS) will be a public conference to discuss, address, and plan for the future of space in Montreal and Canada. MSS will showcase a plethora of recent contributions and advancements in the Canadian space industry.

MSS hopes to provide a platform for leaders to discuss the current state of the space industry in Canada and set out ways to improve it. It will explore current and future issues in these fields in order bring awareness to key areas for improvement. The symposium will also aim to increase exposure and interest from the general population in tackling these issues. Finally, it hopes to empower current active members in the industry and encourage the development of opportunities in for future generations.
